# Module 997 Evidence & Reporting

Security evidence is classified as restricted security metadata. The source
contract allows sanitized case ID, severity, status, incident commander,
business owner, timeline summary, control domain, sanitized impact, decision
record, and review state.

It excludes secret values, credentials, tokens, raw packet data, unredacted
logs, exploit payloads, private topology, customer content, and raw exceptions.

Operational, leadership, customer, and regulatory audiences require separate
views and authorization. Customer and regulatory notices require legal,
compliance, and communication review. External notification and evidence export are disabled.
No evidence store, retention job, or transmission adapter exists.
